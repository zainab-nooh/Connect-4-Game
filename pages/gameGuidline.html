Connect 4 - How to Play

Game Setup

  • 2 Players:2 Players: Player 1 (Blue) vs Player 2 (Red)
  • Objects:Score the most points by making Connect 4 patterns

Playing the Game

1.Taking turns

  • Palyer 1 (Blue) goes first
  • Players alternate turns throughout the game
  • Your name appers when it's your turn

2.Make a Move

  • Click any empty cell on the board to place your disc
  • Once placed, disc stay in that exact position
  • Turn automatically switches to the other player

3.How to Score

  • Horizontal (across) = 1 point
  • Vertical (up/down) = 1 point
  • Diognal (corner to corner) = 1 point

4.Winning the game

  • Game continues until the board is full
  • Winner: Player with the most points(most Connect 4 patterns)
  • Tie: If both players have equal points

Ready to play? Score the most Connect 4 patterns to win!
